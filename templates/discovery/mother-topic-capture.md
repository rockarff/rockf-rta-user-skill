# Mother Topic Capture

Use this module inside Discovery.

Its goal is not to finalize mother topics.  
Its goal is to collect enough raw topic evidence for later Persona and Content work.

## Must Capture

### 1. Repeated Judgment Topics

- What do you repeatedly help people judge?
- What kind of situation makes you reach a conclusion quickly?

### 2. Repeated Misjudgment Topics

- What do people keep getting wrong?
- What repeated misunderstanding do you hear too often?

### 3. Strongest Scene Topics

- What real scene can you describe in detail from memory?
- What kind of conversation, conflict, or decision point do you know very well?

### 4. Strongest Stance Topics

- What issue do you have a clear stance on?
- What do you disagree with in the market?

### 5. Natural Expression Topics

- What can you talk about naturally without notes?
- What topic feels like "your language" instead of forced output?

## Output Format

Collect as five lists:

- repeated_judgment_topics
- repeated_misjudgment_topics
- strongest_scene_topics
- strongest_stance_topics
- natural_expression_topics
