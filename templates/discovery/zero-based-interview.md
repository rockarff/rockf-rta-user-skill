# Zero-Based Interview Script

## Interview Goal

Find the most likely first user group, trust path, and persona direction for a client with little or no validated customer evidence.

## Modules

### 1. Background

- What have you done before this?
- What kind of work gave you the strongest real experience?
- What problems have people already come to you for, even informally?

### 2. Natural Help Zone

- What kind of person do you naturally understand quickly?
- What kind of person feels relieved after talking to you?
- What problem do you explain better than most people around you?

### 3. Strongest Problem Zone

- What kind of problem can you judge quickly?
- What kind of wrong approach do you see people repeat all the time?
- What issue makes you want to say, "This is not how you should handle it"?

### 4. Expression Style

- When you explain things, are you more like a teacher, advisor, judge, companion, or operator?
- Do you naturally speak by logic, emotion, examples, or conclusions first?
- What kind of expression sounds most like you when you are not trying?

### 5. Preferred Future Customer

- If you could only serve one kind of person first, who would it be?
- Who would trust you fastest?
- Who would you most like to keep serving for the next year?

### 6. Likely-To-Close Portrait

- What kind of person feels closest to saying yes, even if they have not paid yet?
- What makes that person hesitate before paying?
- What kind of sentence or situation usually moves that person forward?
- What kind of person looks promising at first but rarely closes in reality?

### 7. Unfit Customer

- Who do you least want to serve?
- What kind of person drains you?
- What kind of customer looks possible on paper but is wrong in reality?

### 8. Mother Topic Seeds

- What kind of problem do you keep wanting to correct when you hear people talk?
- What kind of repeated misunderstanding makes you immediately want to step in?
- What kind of real scene can you describe in detail without preparation?
- What kind of judgment do you make faster than most people around you?
- What kind of topic can you talk about for 10 minutes without forcing it?
