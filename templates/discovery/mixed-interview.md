# Mixed Interview Script

## Interview Goal

Separate validated evidence from forward-looking hypotheses for a client in transition.

## Modules

### 1. Current Stage

- What is already working?
- What is still unclear?

### 2. Real Cases

- Tell me 2-3 real customer cases.
- What patterns are real already?

### 3. Strongest Zone

- What kind of problem do you solve best?
- What kind of person understands your value fastest?

### 4. Future Direction

- If you had to choose one direction to grow next, what would it be?
- Which customer type do you most want to attract next?

### 5. Likely-To-Close Portrait

- Which kind of person feels closest to converting next?
- What usually still blocks them before they pay?
- What exact trigger tends to move them into action?
- Which type looks close but often does not convert?

### 6. Expression Style

- What kind of speaking style feels most natural to you?
- What kind of expression gets the strongest response?

### 7. Trust Logic

- Why do people trust you now?
- Why do the wrong people still come to you?

### 8. Early Market Signals

- What content or conversation already gets response?
- What has market interest but not yet conversion?

### 9. Mother Topic Seeds

- What problem do you already judge repeatedly in real work?
- What repeated misunderstanding do you think the market still has?
- What kind of scene can you already speak about with confidence?
- What kind of topic feels natural for you, even when there is no script?
- Which topics are still hypothesis-only and which are already evidence-backed?
