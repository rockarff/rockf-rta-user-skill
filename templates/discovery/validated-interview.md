# Validated Interview Script

## Interview Goal

Extract real customer evidence from an already operating business.

## Modules

### 1. Business Structure

- What do you sell now?
- What is your current main offer?
- What kind of customer do you hope to grow most?

### 2. Recent Paying Customers

- Describe the last 3 paying customers.
- Where did they come from?
- What did they buy?

### 3. Best-Fit Customers

- Which kind of customer feels easiest to work with?
- Which kind closes fastest?
- Which kind gets the best result?

### 4. Likely-To-Close Customers

- Which kind of person is closest to paying now, even if they have not paid yet?
- What doubt usually remains before they decide?
- What exact trigger usually makes them commit?
- Which kind of person looks close, but usually does not convert?

### 5. Worst-Fit Customers

- Which kind of customer causes the most friction?
- Which kind pays low but consumes a lot?
- Which kind should you stop attracting?

### 6. Trust And Buying

- Why do customers trust you?
- What do they hesitate about?
- What usually makes them finally buy?

### 7. Result And Referral

- What makes customers feel the work was worth it?
- Who repeats?
- Who refers others?

### 8. Content Feedback

- What kind of content has brought the right attention?
- What kind only brought noise?
- What kind of message do people remember from you?

### 9. Mother Topic Seeds

- Across your real cases, what problem do you judge most often?
- What repeated customer misunderstanding do you keep correcting?
- What kind of scene do you explain most vividly in real conversations?
- What kind of stance or judgment makes people remember you fastest?
- If you had to keep speaking on only a few topics for the next 3 months, what would they be?
