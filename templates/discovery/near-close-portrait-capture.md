# Near-Close Portrait Capture

Use this module in Discovery before later USER and CONTENT work.

Its job is to describe the people who are not just "target users", but those most likely to convert next.

## Must Capture

### 1. Fast-Close Type

- What kind of person usually decides fastest?
- What is visible about their identity, pressure, or situation?

### 2. Slow-But-Real Type

- What kind of person hesitates for a while, but still often converts?
- What doubt do they keep carrying before they say yes?

### 3. Final Trigger

- What exact event, realization, sentence, or risk usually moves them forward?
- What makes them stop comparing and start acting?

### 4. False Near-Close Type

- What kind of person looks close, but in reality rarely converts?
- Why do they look promising at first?
- Why do they still not become the right customer?

## Output Format

Collect as four lists:

- fast_close_type
- slow_but_real_type
- final_trigger_patterns
- false_near_close_type
