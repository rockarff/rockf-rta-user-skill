# Material Checklist

## Required

These are the minimum inputs for later work:

1. interview audio or transcript
2. basic business and offer description
3. at least 3 expression samples from the client
4. at least one downstream-customer portrait direction:
   - closed
   - near-close
   - unfit

Near-close portrait evidence should be treated as higher priority than generic target-audience description.

## Recommended

These improve quality significantly:

1. sales conversation screenshots or transcripts
2. WeChat moments copy
3. old content scripts
4. case review notes
5. past customer descriptions
6. descriptions of likely-to-close customers
7. descriptions of people who asked but should not buy
8. what the likely-to-close customer still hesitates about before paying
9. what exact event usually makes that customer finally move

## Bonus

These are optional but valuable:

1. voice notes from consulting calls
2. comments and DMs from content
3. referral stories
4. call recordings from customer objections

## Notes By Client Type

### Zero-Based

Prioritize:

- founder voice
- past work experience
- what kinds of people trusted them before

### Validated

Prioritize:

- real paying cases
- why customers bought
- what good-fit customers look like
- what near-close customers look like
- what bad-fit customers look like
- what near-close customers still need before they decide

### Mixed

Prioritize both:

- existing customer evidence
- future direction evidence
- near-close portrait evidence
- exclusion portrait evidence
- near-close trigger evidence
