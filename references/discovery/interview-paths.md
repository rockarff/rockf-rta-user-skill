# Interview Paths

## Zero-Based Path

Use this when the client has not built enough real customer evidence yet.

Modules:

1. personal background and career path
2. people they naturally help well
3. problems they understand deeply
4. natural speaking and judgment style
5. preferred future customer portrait
6. likely-to-close customer portrait
7. customer they do not want / should not close

Goal:

Build a first working hypothesis for:

- likely user groups
- likely trust path
- likely persona direction
- early broad-traffic portrait
- early semi-vertical receiving portrait

## Validated Path

Use this when the client has enough customer evidence.

Modules:

1. current offer and business structure
2. recent paying customers
3. best-fit customers
4. customers now closest to closing
5. worst-fit customers
6. trust and buying triggers
7. result, repeat purchase, referral
8. existing content feedback

Goal:

Extract:

- real user groups
- real decision barriers
- real trust logic
- real conversion patterns
- already-closed portraits
- ready-to-close portraits
- clearly-unfit portraits

## Mixed Path

Use this when the client has some evidence, but not enough to rely on only the past.

Modules:

1. current business stage
2. small set of real customers
3. strongest problem-solving zone
4. preferred future customer portrait
5. likely-to-close customer portrait
6. unfit customer portrait
7. natural expression style
8. trust-building style
9. early content or market feedback

Goal:

Separate:

- what is already verified
- what is still a hypothesis
- what can already support content targeting
- what still cannot support broad/semi-vertical precision
