# Language Rules

The client-facing version must be:

- short
- clear
- judgment-led
- easy to present aloud

Use:

- conclusion-style titles
- short supporting bullets
- concrete language

Avoid:

- academic tone
- long methodology explanation
- internal jargon
- fluffy consulting language

Preferred title style:

- the most valuable users are not broad traffic, but clear decision-stage users
- the best public-content window sits in the first six stages
- this persona wins by judgment clarity, not by overexplaining
