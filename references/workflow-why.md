# Why This Workflow Exists

## One sentence

This workflow exists to stop downstream content and report work from being built on weak upstream assumptions.

## Why Discovery comes first

Because most projects do not fail at content.

They fail earlier:

- wrong client type
- vague user definition
- no near-close portrait
- no usable mother-topic seed

## Why User comes before Persona

Because expression without a real target user becomes self-description.

Persona should be constrained by:

- who already buys
- who is closest to buying
- who should not be attracted

## Why Persona comes before Content

Because content needs more than knowledge.

It needs:

- a stable speaking identity
- repeatable mother topics
- clear red lines

Without those, topic generation becomes generic.

## Why Workflow is separate

Because most real delivery problems are not "the model cannot write".

They are:

- someone skipped a gate
- someone used weak inputs
- someone does not know what to do next

Workflow exists to:

- judge readiness
- preserve handoff quality
- prevent blind stage-jumping

## Why Report comes last

Because reports should render conclusions, not compensate for missing analysis.

If the upstream logic is weak, a prettier report only hides the weakness.
