# Persona Dimensions

Judge persona through these dimensions:

1. trust role
2. speaking position
3. emotional temperature
4. degree of sharpness
5. degree of decomposition ability
6. user distance
7. authority source
8. conversion posture

Minimum output requirement:

- one stable primary role
- one stable expression direction
- one clear trust path
- three to five recurring topic territories
- three to five red lines

Do not output generic labels such as:

- professional
- reliable
- sincere

unless they are translated into concrete speaking behavior.
