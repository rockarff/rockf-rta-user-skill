# Question Bank

Use these questions selectively. Do not ask all of them at once.

## Business Context

- What do you mainly do now?
- What are your main products or services?
- Which offer do you most want to grow?
- Which offer is most profitable or stable?
- Which type of customer do you want more of?
- Which type of customer do you not want?

## Customer Source

- Where do your customers usually come from?
- What are the last three paying customers like?
- Which customer was easiest to close recently?
- Which customer was hardest to serve recently?
- Which customers came through referral?
- Which customers came through content?

## User Types

- Who may have the problem but has not realized it yet?
- Who has already realized the problem?
- Who is most suitable for your current service?
- Who has the highest payment ability?
- Who is easiest to cooperate with?
- Who is likely to refer others?
- Who is not suitable for you?

## Demand Scenes

- What usually happens before users come to you?
- What situation makes them anxious?
- What do they say in their own words?
- What do they think the problem is?
- What is the real problem behind their words?
- What do they usually misunderstand?
- What happens if they delay?

## Pains

- What are users really afraid of?
- What do they not dare to say directly?
- What consequence do they underestimate?
- What do they repeatedly ask?
- What do they usually search or ask friends about?
- What makes them feel they need a professional?

## Trust And Decision

- What does the user need to see before trusting you?
- What makes users think you are professional?
- What makes users think you understand them?
- What makes users hesitate?
- What makes users compare alternatives?
- What makes users finally decide?
- What causes users to leave without buying?

## Results And Referral

- What result makes users feel satisfied?
- What is a small win for the user?
- What is a big win for the user?
- When do users come back again?
- When do users introduce friends?
- How would users describe you to others?
