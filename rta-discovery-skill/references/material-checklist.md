# Material Checklist

## Required

These are the minimum inputs for later work:

1. interview audio or transcript
2. basic business and offer description
3. at least 3 expression samples from the client

## Recommended

These improve quality significantly:

1. sales conversation screenshots or transcripts
2. WeChat moments copy
3. old content scripts
4. case review notes
5. past customer descriptions

## Bonus

These are optional but valuable:

1. voice notes from consulting calls
2. comments and DMs from content
3. referral stories
4. call recordings from customer objections

## Notes By Client Type

### Zero-Based

Prioritize:

- founder voice
- past work experience
- what kinds of people trusted them before

### Validated

Prioritize:

- real paying cases
- why customers bought
- what good-fit customers look like

### Mixed

Prioritize both:

- existing customer evidence
- future direction evidence
